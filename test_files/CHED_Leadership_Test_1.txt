INTERNATIONAL PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT
Memorandum of Understanding

This document outlines our international partnership with ASEAN universities to champion bold innovation in higher education. We are committed to cultivating global citizens through strategic internationalization efforts.

Key Objectives:
- Champion bold innovation in academic programs
- Cultivate global citizens through international exposure
- Nurture lifelong learning opportunities
- Foster strategic partnerships with international institutions

Partnership Details:
- Collaboration with universities across ASEAN region
- Joint research initiatives in innovative technologies
- Student exchange programs for global citizenship development
- Faculty development programs for international best practices

This partnership demonstrates our commitment to international leadership and innovation in higher education.
